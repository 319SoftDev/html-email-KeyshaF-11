List of things you can do when you turn 18




Vote

At 18, you can legally cast your vote in federal, state, and local elections as long as you are a registered voter and meet applicable voting requirements.



Open a bank account and apply for a credit card

you can now open a bank account in your own name. This is because you the legal age to sign a contract is 18


Get Piercings or Tattoos Without Parental Permission

You can legally get a tattoo or piercing without parental permission, as 18 is the age of legal adulthood for these procedures in the United States


Register to give blood or be an organ donor

By registering as an organ donor at 18, your decision is considered legal consent, so your family cannot override your wishes
